Why we’re fighting to keep the lights on.

What is at stake?

Proximity to our fellow human, togetherness and shared community. Every single live event that we know and love evaporated overnight. With timelines unknown, we must work to ensure these spaces return with a vengeance.


Who is at stake?

Our sisters and brothers in live entertainment that make these experiences possible: The lighting folks, the sound techs, the stage hands, the artists, the small companies, the freelancers….everyone.


What exactly are we doing?

We are calling on artists, small venues, and production members to pull together. The Show Goes On (TheShowGoesOn.Live) is here to serve the this community. The money collected will go to folks in the live event space to ensure that once we kick the shit out of this little virus, we still have the beautiful people supported that keep us inspired.

Here’s HOW the show will go on.

Great shows from your favorite artists live streamed. Merch and other products and services will be available throughout each live streamed session.
